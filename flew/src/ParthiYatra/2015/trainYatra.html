Train Parthi Yatra - 2015

(August 7th - 8th - 9th)

Aum Sri Sairam, We the devotes of Erode District, Tamil Nadu are offering our loving Pranams at the divine lotus feet of our mother Sai. On the Eve of Bhagwans 90th Birthday celebrations, Swami has blessed us to undertake our Second Pavitra Parthi Yatra for a duration of three Days from August 7th to August 9th of the year 2015. It is worth mentioning here that, for the second consecutive time, our district is undertaking Parthi Yatra by a special charted train.
