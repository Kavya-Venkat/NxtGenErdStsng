Sai values in Ramayana & Ramakatha - Parthi Yatra - 2015

(August 8th)

Swami has blessed the devotees of Erode district to undertake our Second consecutive Pavitra Parthi Yatra. The second day evening program at Sai Kulwant Hall had the balavikas children of Erode District from Jaycess School were offering a drama entitled Sai values in Ramayana, lessons drawn from Ramakatha Rasavani, which also contained a Ramakatha Rendition in Parthi style.



Truth is His name, love is His Breath, Dharma is His voice, His presence is Peace, His hearet is state of non-violence. Ramayan is not a story of an individual, it is a story of the universe. The story does not deal with the past, present or future, but with the timeless eternal truth. It is an illuminating, inspiring human drama which promotes us to follow the path of Sai Values Sathyam, Dharmam, Shanthi, Prema and Ahimsa. The five values from Ramayana are being encated through episodes based on the divine Sai values by the Balavikas students of Jaycees School, Erode.

The program starts with an invocation dance on divine love. It is the divine love that gives us strength, wisdom, knowledge and salvation. It is the divine love that is true and permanent. The dance is followed by Ramakath rendition in Parthi style and episodes from Ramayana on Sathyam, Dharmam, Shanthi, Prema and Ahimsa. Jai Sairam.
