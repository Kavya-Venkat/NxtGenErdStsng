SRI SATHY SAI SPECIAL - Train Parthi Yatra - 2012

(August 17th - 18th - 19th)

Swami has blessed the devotees of Erode district to undertake our First Pavitra Parthi Yatra which also marks the 6th Annual Sadhana Camp of Erode Past Balvikas Alumni.

The uniqueness of this Pavitra Parthi Yatra is that, for the first time ever in the history of Prashanti Nilayam, that a district is undertaking Parthi Yatra by a special charted train "SRI SATHYA SAI SPECIAL".

The train had about 20 coaches consisting of 108 2nd seater seats each. This chartered train was beautifully docorated with the Engine majestically hosting our belowed Bhagwans photo. The train reached Erode junction in the early hours of 17th August 2012 and after perfoming aarti to our Mother Sai at the junction, with all the devotees boarding the train, the journey began at around 10am. The train reached Sri Sathya Sai Prashanthi Nilayam junction by 9pm the same day.

After having had a beautiful darshan of our belowed Bhagwan at Prashanthi Nilayam, and visiting the holy places of significance at Puttaparthi, the return journy from the abode of peace started by 6pm on 19th August2012, the Sunday.
