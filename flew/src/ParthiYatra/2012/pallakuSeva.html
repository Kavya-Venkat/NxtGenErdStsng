Pallaku Seva - Parthi Yatra - 2012

(August 19th)

Swami has blessed the devotees of Erode district to undertake our First Pavitra Parthi Yatra which also marks the 6th Annual Sadhana Camp of Erode Past Balvikas Alumni.

After Sunday Morning Mandir Bhajans on August 19th, the devotees from the Erode district were blessed to offer Pallaku Seva for the Lord of the Universe, Bhagawan Sri Sathya Sai Baba. The Pallaku Seva was inaugrated by Respected Sri. Ratnakar Garu, Truestee, Sri Sathya Sai Central Trust.

The Pallaku containing our Lord with beautiful garlands in all its Majesty was grandly escorted by Vedam, Bhajans, Nadaswaram and Thavil starting. Pallaku Seva started from Kalyanamantap and culminated at Ganesha statue with Mangala Aarti being offered to Bhagawan. We express our obeisance and prayers for blessing us to offer Pallaku Seva to our Merciful Lord Sai.
