Symposium on Sai Philosophy

Aum Sri Sai Ram. The main event of the 9th Annual Sadhana Camp is the "Symposium on Sai Philosophy".

We had 6 topics identified on various aspects of personality of Bhagawan Baba which we as humans can inspire, aspire and follow to make a worthy living. Each topic had 3 speakers with 18 speeches in total. Symposium on Sai Philosophy was moderated by our senior alumni Sri. Sayee Manohar and Sri. Karuppiah.
