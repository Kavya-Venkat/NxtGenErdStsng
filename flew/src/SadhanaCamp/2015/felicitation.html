Felicitation For The Nithya Sai Sevaks

Aum Sri Sai Ram. Swami says "Gratitude is a great attitude" and Swami has always acknowledged the gratitude program offerred by the students passing out of the Sri Sathya Sai University every year across the campuses. Taking it to the district of erode, for teh first time in the Annual Sadhana Camp, Felicitation For The Nithya Sai Sevaks was being conducted recognising the selfless service rendered by Sai Sevaks across decades to Swami and SSSSO, Erode District in every capacity.
