Flag Hoisting, Pallaku Seva, Jaya Vijayee Bhava.

Aum Sri Sai Ram. With the divine blessings of our most belowed Bhagawan, Sri Sathya Sai Balavikas Alumni and Alumni of Sri Sathya Sai University from the Sri Sathya Sai Seva Organizations, Erode District welcome you all to this grand occassion of 9th Annual Sadhana Camp. 1st day of Sadhana Camp started off with the Prashanthi Flag hoisting, with Flag song at the background. This was followed by pallakku utsavam with the pallakku song in the background. Swami was then worshipped with rendition of Jaya Vijayee Bhava.
