Valedictory Function.

Aum Sri Sai Ram. With the divine blessings of our most belowed Bhagawan, the 9th Annual Sadhana Camp came to a grand conclusion with Valedictory Function.

The valedictory function had the Welcome address by Smt. S. R. Parvathy, Deputy District Educational Coordinator. This was followed by Presidential Address by Smt. M. Geetha, Deputy District Educational Coordinator.

The Valedictory Address was given by Sri. R. Guru Ragavendran, District Educational Coordinator, Erode. Sadhana Camp winding up note was delivered by Sri. A. Padmanaba Ganesan, Deputy District Educational Coordinator.

Vote of Thanks was then given by the Spiritual Coordinator, Palayapalayam Samithi, Sri. S. Nagarajan.
