Quiz Program - Alumni Children and Current Balavikas Students

Aum Sri Sai Ram. Quiz program was conducted to the II & III group balavikas children of Erode District and children of Past Balavikas alumni. Questions on the life of Sathya Sai Baba and from topics of the Indian scriptures of Mahabharatha and Ramayana was asked. It was an engaging session after the lunch which set the tone for the upcoming afternoon sessions.
