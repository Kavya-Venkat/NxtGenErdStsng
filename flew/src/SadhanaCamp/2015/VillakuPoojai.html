Kuthu Vilakku Poojai.

Aum Sri Sai Ram. The main event of the 3rd day of the 9th Annual Sadhana Camp is the Kuthu Vilakku Poojai, by the Mahila members of Erode District SSSO. It was an eye catchy exhibition of the golden shaded vilakkus decorated with colorful flowers across the entire auditorium with mahila members chanding the ashtothram and offering their obeisance and salutations to our Mother Sai. More than 100 mahila members took part in one of the most awaited event.
