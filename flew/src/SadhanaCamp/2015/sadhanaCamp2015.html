Sadhana Camp - 2015

(July 17th - 19th)

Aum Sri Sai Ram. With the divine blessings of our most belowed Bhagawan, Sri Sathya Sai Balavikas Alumni and Alumni of Sri Sathya Sai University from the Sri Sathya Sai Seva Organizations, Erode District welcome you all to this grand occassion of 9th Annual Sadhana Camp for a duration of three days from July 17th to July 19th of the year 2015. This year also marks the celebrations of the 90th year of the advent of Bhagawan Sri Sathya Sai Baba. With utmost gratitude we offer our humble salutations at His Lotus Feet and invoke His blessings for this Sadhana Camp.
