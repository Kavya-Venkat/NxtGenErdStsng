Sadhana Camp - 2017

(July 29th - 30th)

Aum Sri Sai Ram. With the divine blessings of our most belowed Bhagawan, Sri Sathya Sai Balavikas Alumni and Alumni of Sri Sathya Sai University from the Sri Sathya Sai Seva Organizations, Erode District welcome you all to this grand occassion of 11th Annual Sadhana Camp, "Iraivan Sri Sathya Sai Sangeetha Kala Vaibhavam" for a duration of two days from July 29th to July 30th of the year 2017. With utmost gratitude we offer our humble salutations at His Lotus Feet and invoke His blessings for this Sadhana Camp.
