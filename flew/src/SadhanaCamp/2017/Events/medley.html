Aum Sri Sai Ram. Medley means that they are taken from different varieties. This Sai Medley Bhajan offering consists of songs composed by Bhagavan Baba and sung at several occasions.

Our Balavikas Children, Sai Gayatri, Chaya Krishnan, Nidhisree, Shankari, Padmavathi, Sai Lakshmi, Mounika and Devadharshini are singing these songs to offer their love to our Swami.

They are trained by our belowed Shyam Anna. Mr. Shyam is a former student of Sri Sathya Sai University.

This program will definitely give all of us the blessings of Lord Bhagavan at different times. The children who performed are felicitated by Mrs. Shyam with the Memorial Prize of Bhagawan. Our Belowed Sai Anna felicitated Shyam Anna for his noble efforts in training the budding singers of Erode District.
