Part - 2




We are here to witness the unique celebration of Sri Sathya Sai Jananotsavam. On the merit of the incessant tapas of several Sages, Rishis and the continuous prayers of devotees, that our Bhagawan Sri Sathya Sai Baba descended down in His avataric mission to show the humanity the path of Love. As a mark of the avataric manifestation, during and after midnight, the Tambura, the Tabela and other musical instruments started playing on its own giving mellifluous musical treat to the astonishment and amusement of everyone present. A saint explained the significance of this event to be the one which will bring good omen, an avataric descend for the greater good, peace and harmony of the entire humanity.

It is this significant incident that we are going to see live over here. Initially, we will see a small clip from the famaous Tele-Serial by Smt. Anjali Devi, "Shirdi Sai, Parthi Sai" over the LED projector. It will be followed the celebration of the naming ceremony where our Mahila members and members of our Sai family over the stage.
