Aum Sri Sai Ram. The next program that we are going to witness is “Sayee Gaanam”, performed by Smt. Shantala Subramaniam.

Smt. Shantala Subramaniam was trained in music from an early age in Chennai. She is the sister of the famous and the most talented flutist, Sri Shashank. She has travelled the lenght and bredth of the world giving musical feast to millions and has been honored with many awards in the field of music.

Supporting Artist for the programme are the well knowm Ms. Akkarai Swarnalatha in Violin, Sri Palakad Mahesh in Mirudangam and Sri Govind Prasad in Moorsingh. Come on, let us sit back and get drenched in the rain of music.
