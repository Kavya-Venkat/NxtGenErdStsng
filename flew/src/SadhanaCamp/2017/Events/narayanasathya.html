Let us now listen to the avataric greatness of our SathyaNarayana, Bhagawan Sri Sathya Sai Baba, through the nectarine rendition of Narayana Sathya Narayana. Our well known Musical Maestros Late Shri T.M.SoundharRajan and Late Shri Malaysia Vasudevan were strong devotees of our Bhagawan Baba. Once Swami has appeared as Lord Muruga to Shri T.M.SoundharRajan. Infact, Shri Malaysia Vasudevan did realize the importance and significance of Prashanthi Nilayam as an abode of peace for the entire humanity sans discrimination based on country, caste, creed and religion. He was blessed to sing infront of Bhagawan a song whose meaning goes thus, "When Swami's face is there in front of us, why do we need another one?".

Now we are going to have our Balavikas Children sing the songs of Shri.TMS and Malaysia Vasudevan which were rendered in the divine presence of our belowed Bhagawan.

Worshipping our Swami and calling out with devotion as Kanna, following Balavikas children will be singing the glory of our divine Master as Narayana, our SathyaNarayana.
