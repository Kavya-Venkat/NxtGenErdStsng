Aum Sri Sai Ram. The next program is Sri Sathy Sai Ashtothara Vidanana Chathurveda Shodasopara Pooja. Shodasopachara consists of performing of sixteen types of worshipping the diety. In the tenets of Sanatana Dharma, performing pooja forms a most important aspect in the worship of God.Performing pooja consists of getting the body and mind purified and thus performing pooja for the God.The sixteen forms of workship begins with meditation (Dhyana) and proceeds through invocation of the deity (Avahanam), offering the sacred water and culminates with waving of the camphor to the deity.

This ritual is performed by Sri. Raguraman Sastrigal, Erode, a former Balvikas student of Erode Samithy. He has got trained in the Vedam chanting from Kumbakonam Veda Patashala for 8 years.
