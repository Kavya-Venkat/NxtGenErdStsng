"Key Note address - Shri G.Varadhan, State President, SSSO, Tamilnadu"


Key Note Address was given by our respected Shri. G.Varadhan, State President, SSSO - TamilNadu. He praised all the Balavikas Alumni, Alumni of Sathya Sai University, District Office bearers and members of SSSO, Erode district for unparallel commitment shown towards all the service activities. He made a special mention to document all undertaken service activities given the enormous amount of activities undertaken at Erode district for posterity and to remain as an exemplar district for all the other districts across India to follow the path and raise up.

The Key Note and Welcome Address was followed by the Divine discourse of our most belowed Bhagawan.
