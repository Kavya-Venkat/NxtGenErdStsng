Aum Sri Sairam. The next in line is the most eagerly awaited musical presentation by Smt. NithySree Mahadevan and party titled “Sai Thamarai Padangal”.

Smt. NithyaSree Mahadevan has been spreading carnatic music throughout the world since 1987. Awarded with so many coveted titles and awards, she was also awarded with honorary doctrate by Sathyabama University in the year 2008 for her service to the music industry. An ardent devotee of Bhagawan, she has sung in His physical presence several times and has been recipient of Swami’s grace. She will be accompanied by M.A. KrishnaSwamy on Violin and Sri. Sivakumar on Mridangam.

We extend a warm welcome to Smt. NithySree Mahadevan and party and let us all bask in her music for the next 90 minutes.
