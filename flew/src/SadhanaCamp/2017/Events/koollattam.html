Pallakku Seva & Kolaattam



Aum Sri Sai Ram. The current program is the Pallaku Seva. In this auspicious offering to the Divine, we bring Bhagawan by carrying Him in pallaku which is escorted by Kolattam performed by the Gandhi Nagar Samithi Balavikas Children. Come let us bring the Lord. After Pallaku Seva, Smt. Usha Muralidharan felicitated the kids who have performed kolattam.
