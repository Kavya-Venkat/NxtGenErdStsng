Prashanthi Flag hoisting is followed by District Office bearers meeting headed by our respected District President of Erode District, Shri. D.Rajendran. Initially he outlined the various service, spiritual and educational activities undertaken at Erode District with emphasis on School Balavikas. Smt. Sarasvathi then outlined the importance of School Balavikas, the practical difficulties faced and the presence of Swami's divine hand throughout. This was followed by Smt. Ramya Ramanan and Smt. Lakshmi Saravanan sharing their unique experinces of taking School balavikas for which they travel every week from Bangalore to Erode, marvelous is their love, service and dedication indeed!

District president then gave an overview on the balavikas activities at our adopted villages of Kadaganalli and Pavalakkoattai and highlighted the contributios of our District Service Coordinator, Shri. Jayachandran.

Shri. Balakrishnan then gave a small overview of the unity of the three wings, Spiritual wing, Service Wing and Educational wing of Sri Sathya Sai Seva Organizations, Erode District. Infact he said that, Swami was partial in His blessings ragarding this unity to Erode district which acts as an example to severa other districts in TamilNadu and India at large.

This was followed by the inaugration of Sri Sathya Sai Sthree Dharma Bodhini (SSSSDB), for the first time ever in TamilNadu. District Educational Coordinator Shri. Guru Raghavendran gave an overview of SSSSDB and highlighted the important role of Mahila members at Erode district who form the pillar of strength in all the undertaken service activities. Smt. Maheswari Rajendran inaugrated the event. As a part of SSSSDB, Student of Primary School, Kum. Sai Sruthi spoke about the importance of being born as a women and responsibilities being carried over by women in upholding the morality in society. Kum. Narmadha gave a short overview of the importance of women and the Varalakshmi pooja. Finally, Kum. SriLatha elucidated the greatness of motherhood.

Audiene then headed for Lunch.
