Aum Sri Sai Ram. In this Sangeetha Kala Vaibhavam, the next music concert is "Sai Sangeerthanam" by Shri Sai Prasad, Shri Gagan Dalvi, Shri Sayeesh Malpani, Shri Anurag Guru & Shri Karthik Ramakrishnan and team.

  • Sri Gagan Talvi - He is a former student of our Satya Mirpuri Music College and has also been as Prashanti mandir Bhajan singer in Puttaparthi for 5 years.
  • Sri Saish Malpani - Sitar Music Artist. He hails from Pune, Maharashtra. He is a former Balavikas student, who has completed 5 years of Diploma in Music at Sri Sathya Sai Mirpuri College of Music.
  • Sri Anurag Guru, Renowned Tabla Artist, from Madhya Pradesh. He is a former student of Balavikas, who has completed Diploma in Music for six years at Sri Sathya Sai Mirpuri Music College. He has gone to many countries to give music concerts.
  • Sri Sai Prasad, he is a past Balavikas student from erode, and is a Carnatic singer. A Student of Sri Sathya Sai Mirpuri Music College of Music, he learnt Carnatic music for 2 years and has been blessed to perform infront of the Divine.
  • Karthick Ramakrishnan - Harmonist and Casio Player. He is student of Brindavan Campus of Sri Sathya Sai Institure of Higher Learning, and a past Balavikas Student of Erode. He was the recepient of Gold Medal for the Masters Program in Financial Management at Swamis University.

Our brothers have made Hindustani music a wonderful and exciting event at this musical party. Sri Thangavelu felicitated the participants with the Memorial Prize of Bhagavan.Lets enjoy the rhythm of our brothers.
