Aum Sri Sai Ram. The current program we are blessed with is Sarvam Sayee Mayam.

This is going to be performed by Sri Neelakantan & Sri Sai Rakshith and Team. Isnt everything in this world Sayee Mayam!!, yes infact, and through this program we will realize the same.

Sri Neelakantan is a very talented flutist who studied BSc, MSc and MTech in Sri Sathya Sai Institute of Higher Learning and was blessed with a lot of opportunities infront Bhagawan Sri Sathya Sai Baba.

Sai Rakshith is a child prodigy who has explored and shown his talent over the stringed instrument Violin at various places and infront of the Divine presence.

Sri Balakrishnan felicitated the participants with the Memorial Prize of Bhagawan.
