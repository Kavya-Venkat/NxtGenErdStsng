Aum Sri Sai Ram. Unity in Sathya Sai, Isai Amudam by Kum.Kavya, Kum.Bharathi, Smt.Sailakshmi and Team.

The leaves are different, but their color is only one (Green). Though religions are different, God is One and to drive home this point, our Lord says, "Bangaram (Gold) may be different but the Jewelry is one." Unity in Sathya Sai, is one such program which will remind us the same. To every ones attention, these songs were blessed and given by our belowed Swami Himself to one German Lady in her dream, to be performed in the World Conference that hapenned at Puttaparthi.

This program also contains songs like Love is my form, Why fear when I am Here, which are sung by our belowed Swami and some more English songs which are sung in front of the Divine.

This program is presented by Kum. Kavya, Kum. Bharati, Kum. Preeti and Smt. Sai Lakshmi.

This performance will for sure stay in our ears and minds forever.
