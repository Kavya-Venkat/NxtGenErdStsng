Valedictory Address



Aum Sri Sai Ram. Valedictory Address is given by Shri. R. Gururaghavendran, District Educational Co-ordinator, Erode.

With the infinite blessings of Bhagawan Sri Sathya Sai Baba, 11th Sadhana Camp took place extremely well. We pray to Bhagawan to shower choicest blessings on all the participants and members of Erode Sai Family. Sai Anna has been a pillar of support and backbone for all the undertaken efforts at Erode. We thank Bhagawan for guiding us through Sai Anna. We would also like to thank our loving District President for supporting all the undertaken efforts at Erode by the Past Balavikas Alumni. The Sadhana Camp would come to a close after the Valedictory Address with Managala Aarti.

Jai Sai Ram !!
