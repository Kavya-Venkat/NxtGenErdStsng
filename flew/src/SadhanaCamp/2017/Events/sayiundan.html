Aum Sri Sai Ram. Upcoming program is “Sayee Unthan Tirunamam” which is based on Sai Geetamulu” This was written in Telugu, Comprising of 120 Telugu songs and 15 tamil songs which are composed and written and even sung by our Swami. From this a few songs are going to be sung by Mrs. Geeta Ramadas and the crew.

Mrs. Geeta Ramadas's daughter, while studying Balavikas, got the great opportunity to be present at Puttaparthi on behalf of Mumbai Samithi in front of Bhagavan on the auspicious Ashada Ekadasi Day.

It is our great fortune that Mrs. Geetha Ramadas is serving as the expert trainer for the state level group singing competition for children in Erode district, Moolapalayam Samithy. Also she is giving bhajan training for the Mahilas of our district. She is not only proficient in Carnatic Music but also in Hindustani Music. Let us invite them to the stage to give their offering to our Lord.
