Aum Sri Sai Ram. We are here to witness Sai Yoga Dance, here yogasanas are performed in a dance form offering their love to the lord.

For performing this kids from Therkupallam, Pazhalayapalayam, Moolapalayam, Solar, Soolai, Balaji Garden samithi children are participating in this event, they have been practising hard, completely offering their body,mind and soul to the same. Our belowed yoga master, Yogacharya Sri Gopalakrishnan, who has introduced the yoga to our balavikas children, has been training them for months together for this program.

Taking inspiration from the kids who performed, we should also practise this in our daily life.

After the Sai Yoga Dance Programme, Yogacharya Sri Gopalakrishnan was invited to the stage to receive memorial gift of Bhagavan as a Prasad.

Also Mrs. Gopi Visalakshi was invited to the stage to give them the memorial gift of bhagawan to Ms. Narmadha and Mrs. Parvathi. District Mahila Co-ordinator Mrs. Leelawati gave commemorative gifts to the children who performed yoga dance.
