Aum Sri Sai Ram. Kovil Kondanadi Parthiyile - A Bharathanatiyam program by Swasti School of Dance and Lakshmi Garden Samithi balavikas children, Erode.

Ms. Sankavi Ganesh is the incharge for this classical dance who has trained the children completely. This dance program is offered to our Lord by Ms. Mahathi and the team who is a balavikas student.

Over the last years, the dance team has been conducting dance performances in various locations. “Swasthi Dance School” takes pride and considers it a great fortune and opportunity in staging this dance "Kovil Kondanadi Parthiyile", to our belowed Bhagawan who is residing in all our hearts, which is our temple, Parthi, the Very Prashanti Nilayam!!
