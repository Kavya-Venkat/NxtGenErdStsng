Aum Sri Sai Ram. “Sayee Paadal Thogupugal” - A Saxophone concert performed by Dr. M. Jayakumar and team.

Saxophone is one instrument, which has the ability to force people to be on their toes, and elevate them into the higher dimensions of happiness and music instantly. It brings the child out in all of us. The music from Saxophone can attract the musical interest even to person not familiar with music.

Dr. Jayakumar, who has been entertaining the people with Saxophone for about 23 years has gracefully accepted our request to offer his talent at Swamis lotus feet today and thereby immerse us all in the flood of music. In the year 2006, he was conferred the prestigious Doctorate in the Unites States of America by the World Tamil University.

Let us sit back and enjoy the scintilating music emanating from the ever cheerful instrument, Saxophone.
