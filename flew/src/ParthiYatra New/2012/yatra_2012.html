Parthi Yatra - 2012

(August 17th - 18th - 19th)

Aum Sri Sairam, We the devotes of Erode District, Tamil Nadu are offering our loving Pranams at the divine lotus feet of our mother Sai. On the Eve of Bhagvans 87th Birthday, Swami has blessed us to undertake our First Pavitra Parthi Yatra for a duration of three Days from August 17th to August 20th of the year 2012. This holy occasion also marks the 6th Annual Sadhana Camp of our Erode Past Balvikas Alumni.

It is worth mentioning here that, for the first time ever in the history of Prashanti Nilayam, that a district is undertaking Parthi Yatra by a special charted train, "SRI SATHYA SAI SPECIAL".

As a part of this Pavitra Parthi Yatra, around 2000 devotes which includes 1008 Balavikas children took part reaching Prashanti Nilayam through the charted train from Erode offering their humble salutations to Swami.The balavikas group consists of Rural, School, Residential and regular Balvikas children. More than 80% of those BV children and most of the accompanying teachers and balavikas gurus are visiting Prashanthi Nilayam for the 1st time. Infact, half of those balavikas children have never travelled in any train (mainly rural centers).

In this Pavitra Parthi Yatra, devotees were taken to the various places inside the Puttaparthi such as Chaitanya Jyothi, Eswaramma Samadhi, Swami's birth place, Venugopal Swami temple, Anjaneya temple, Gokulam, Sathya Sai museum etc.

Our Balvikas children will be offering their Bhajans infront of Bhagwan today evening, 18th August at 1715 hrs. Tomorrow we are offering a Pallaku Seva for Bhagwan starting from Kalyanamantapam to Ganesha statue.

Once again i pray to our most merciful Mother Sai, to shower HIS choicest blessings and grace on every one of us and guide us continuously in making this holy pilgrimage a most successful and meaningful one.
