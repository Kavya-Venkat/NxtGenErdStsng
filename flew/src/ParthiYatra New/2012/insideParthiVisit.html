Inside Puttaparthi Visit - Parthi Yatra - 2012

(August 17th - 18th - 19th)

Swami has blessed the devotees of Erode district to undertake our First Pavitra Parthi Yatra which also marks the 6th Annual Sadhana Camp of Erode Past Balvikas Alumni.

During the Parthi Yatra from August 17th till 19th, various places of significance inside Prashanti Nilayam Ashram and Puttaparthi were visited. It is to be noted that, as a part of this Pavitra Parthi Yatra, around 2000 devotes which includes 1008 Balavikas children took part from Erode district. The balavikas group consists of Rural, School, Residential and regular Balvikas children. More than 80% of those BV children and most of the accompanying teachers and balavikas gurus are visiting Prashanthi Nilayam for the 1st time.

The places visited includes Chaitanya Jyothi, Eswaramma Samadhi, Swami's birth place, Venugopal Swami temple, Anjaneya temple, Gokulam, Sathya Sai museum, Hill View Stadium, Shanti Vedika, Sri Sathya Sai Books and Publications, General Stores etc.

After having had a beautiful darshan of our belowed Bhagwan at Prashanthi Nilayam, and visiting the holy places of significance at Puttaparthi, the return journy from the abode of peace started by 6pm on 19th August2012, the Sunday.
