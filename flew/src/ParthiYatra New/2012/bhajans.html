Bhajans & Samadhi Group Photos - Parthi Yatra - 2012

(August 18th)

Swami has blessed the devotees of Erode district to undertake our First Pavitra Parthi Yatra which also marks the 6th Annual Sadhana Camp of Erode Past Balvikas Alumni.

The Erode youth and balavikas children were blessed by Bhagawan to offer Mandir bhajans on August 18th during our First Parthi Yatra. Immediately after the Veda chanting, the Bhajans commenced with Ganesha song as usual followed by series of songs on various forms of Lord Sai between 5.15pm and 5.45pm. We are ever grateful to Swami for this opportunity as it is a dream come true for any aspiring singer to offer to his devotion at Sai Kulwant Hall in thy Divine presence.

After Bhajans and Mangala Aarti, Bhagawan blessed the Singers, District Office bearers, Bhajana Mandali and Samithy coordinators and Balavikas Gurus of Erode district for a group photo infront of the Samadhi. We express our heartfelt gratitude and salutations to our ever merciful mother Sai.
