Day 2 - Manasa Bhajare Guru Charanam


Aum Sri Sai Ram. The youth & Balvikas Children from the Sri Sathya Sai Organizations, Erode District, place themselves at the lotus feet of our beloved God and Jagadguru, Bhagawan Sri Sathya Sai Baba, the avatar of the age who descended down to save the humanity from the clutches of Adharma.

Guru is the one who removes the ignorance and illumines the intelligence, who is not limited by attributes and not affected by appearances. He is free from prejudices and treats everyone with equal love. This is the love all of us experience day in and day out with our beloved lord who is the Jagadguru and the Lord of Universe.
We the youth and Balvikas children humbly offer at His Lotus Feet, this drama entitled “Manasa Bhajare Guru Charanam”.

Swami declares, "I have come to light the lamp of love in your hearts, the universal unitary Faith that makes the atmic principle shine forth day by day with added luster". For that, He beckons all of us to mediate upon "Manasa Bhajare Guru Charanam, Dustara Bhava Sagara Tharanam - Oh! Mind! to cross the turbulent ocean of life, sing the glory of the Divine Preceptor and surrender at His Lotus Feet".
Mind?!! Swami addresses the youth of today as Mind.

This drama "MANASA BHAJARE GURU CHARANAM" opens with a scene of a Balvikas class, where the balvikas guru is discussing about the project work taken up by the third group balvikas students on the theme "MANASA BHAJARE GURU CHARANAM", which carries major thrust on the AdiShankaracharya's BhajaGovindam.
