Pavitra Parthi Yatra - 2018

(June 15th - 16th - 17th)

When the whole world is in celebration of the 93rd year of the advent of our Iraivan Sri Sathya sai, we the erode district sai devotees about more then 2000 in number, have gathered in the sacred sai kulwant hall, at His abode with prayerful gratitude, as a part of the third pavitra parthi yatra from June 15th to June 17th of the year 2018, for a period of three days.

This holy occasion also marks the 12th Annual Sadhana Camp of our Erode Past Balvikas Alumni which includes alumni of Sri Sathya Sai University too.



With the pure thought of involving all the past balavikas families in sathsang who are united by the undercurrent of the Love of our mother Sai, more than a decade ago in the year 2007, the past balavikas alumni from Erode district, started the annual sadhana camp at Erode.

What was started as a small effort, has grown over years into what it is today, where all the Sai families across all the samithies and balavikas centres from the length and breadth of erode district and its regions take part, rejoicing the glory of our Iraivan Sri Sathya sai.

It is heartening to know that, the baton of the Sadhana camp is passed on to the second, third and so on to the generations of balavikas alumni, which reinstates the faith that, the Sadhana camp will go on and on for eternity. As a part of this pavitra parthi yatra, we are presented the drama Manasa Bhajare Guru Charanam with a thrust on Bhajagovindam, in parthi style. As a part of this drama, we also be enacted Iraivan Sri Sathya Sai Jananotsavam. The presentation involved balavikas children, youth and alumni of Sathya Sai Institute of Higher learning, totaling 108, a sacred number. On the day two of this Divine yatra contained another musical drama presentation named "Sthri Dharma Bothini",which described about the strength and values the sthree's(women).
